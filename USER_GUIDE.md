# 🎯 Geofence App: User Guide

Welcome to your Geofence App! This guide will help you set up and use the application to track your location and receive alerts when you enter or exit specific zones.

---

## 1. Getting Started: Permissions
The app requires two specific permissions to work correctly, especially in the background:
*   **Location Access**: Set this to **"Always Allow"** (not just "While Using App") so the app can monitor zones even when your phone is in your pocket.
*   **Notifications**: Allow these so you receive an alert the moment you enter or leave a zone.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If you skip these during the initial setup, you can enable them later in your phone's **Settings > Geofence App**.

---

## 2. Using the Map
The map is your main interface for managing zones:
*   **Blue Icon**: Represents your current real-time GPS position.
*   **Red Pin/Circle**: Represents a geofence zone you have created.
*   **My Location Button**: Tap the 🎯 icon in the top right to instantly center the map on your current position.

---

## 3. Adding a Geofence
Creating a new tracking zone is simple:
1.  **Find the spot** on the map where you want to add a zone.
2.  **Long-press or Tap** the map at that exact location.
3.  An **"Add Geofence"** dialog will appear.
4.  Enter a **Name** (e.g., "Home" or "Office") and a **Radius** in meters (default is 100m).
5.  Tap **Add**. A red circle will now appear on the map.

---

## 4. Automatic Tracking
The app is designed to be "always-on":
*   **Zero Setup**: As soon as you add a geofence, tracking starts internally. There are no buttons to press.
*   **Continuous Monitoring**: The app will monitor your position against your zones in the background even if you close the app or lock your screen.

---

## 5. Receiving Alerts
When tracking is active:
*   As you walk or drive into a red circle, you will receive a notification: **"Entered Zone: [Name]"**.
*   When you move out of the circle, you will receive: **"Exited Zone: [Name]"**.
*   These work even if the app is minimized or the screen is locked.

---

## 6. Managing your Zones
*   **Delete a Zone**: Scroll to the "Geofences" list at the bottom and tap the **Trash Icon** next to the name.
*   **Pan to Zone**: Tap any geofence name in the list to instantly move the map camera to that zone.
*   **Stop Tracking**: To pause background monitoring, simply remove the geofences you no longer wish to track from the list at the bottom.
